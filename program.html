Additional Information

Program Notes

Hackathon Projects

Project ideas and team formation will begin the morning of Day 1. Participants are encouraged to come with ideas, datasets, or half-formed tools they want to develop.

Computing Resources

Details on computing access and datasets will be shared with registered participants in advance of the hackathon.

Presentations

Each team will give a short presentation on Day 3 describing what they built, what worked, and what comes next. Slides and code will be shared with the community.
